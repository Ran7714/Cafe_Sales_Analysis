# Cafe_Sales_Analysis
End-to-end Cafe Sales Analysis using Python, Pandas, SQL, and Matplotlib. Performed data cleaning, EDA, visualization, and business insight generation to identify sales trends, top-selling items, revenue patterns, and customer payment preferences.

☕ Cafe Sales Analysis | End-to-End Data Analytics Project

Transforming raw cafe transaction data into actionable business insights through data cleaning, exploratory analysis, SQL, and an interactive Power BI dashboard.

📖 Overview

Businesses collect large volumes of sales data every day, but raw data alone doesn't support decision-making. This project demonstrates a complete data analytics workflow by converting raw café transaction records into meaningful insights that help understand sales performance, customer preferences, and product trends.

The project includes data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis (EDA), business insight generation, and dashboard development using Power BI.

🎯 Business Problem

The café wants to understand:

Which products generate the highest revenue?
How do sales change over time?
Which products are most popular?
Which payment methods do customers prefer?
Which products perform best each month?

The objective is to answer these questions using data and provide recommendations that can support better business decisions.

🔍 Key Insights
📈 October recorded the highest monthly sales.
🥗 Salad generated the highest overall revenue.
🛒 Customer demand remained relatively consistent throughout the year.
💳 Digital payment methods accounted for a significant share of transactions.
⭐ High-demand products contributed the majority of total revenue.
💼 Business Recommendations
Increase inventory during peak sales periods.
Promote low-performing products through bundle offers.
Continue investing in digital payment options.
Prioritize high-revenue products in marketing campaigns.
